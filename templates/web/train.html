IRCTC Train Ticket Booking Online

    Here's how to book IRCTC tickets online:
  • :Open the ixigo website or download the mobile app and go to the homepage under the trains section.
  • :Select the source and destination Indian Railway stations from the list.
  • :Pick a date of departure and click on search trains.
  • :Also, select your travel class from the available classes - AC First Class, Sleeper Class, Second AC, Third AC or AC Chair car.
  • :Choose your Indian Railway train and travel class based on arrival time and travel time (in hours).
  • :Fill in the passenger details (including children, adults and senior citizens) and choose your berth preferences.
  • :Log in using your IRCTC user ID and password. Create a new IRCTC username if you don't have one.
  • :Complete the payment and reservation process using your IRCTC login details. You will be redirected to the IRCTC website.
  • :You will then receive a confirmation on your registered email ID and SMS on mobile number.
