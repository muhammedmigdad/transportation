Yatra Money

Yatra has now launched its virtual currency which is known as Yatra money. Users can both earn and spend Yatra money on their flight and bus bookings. Since there is no limit on how much Yatra money you can spend on one booking, users can use 100% of their Yatra money balance on a single flight booking and upto ₹150 on a bus booking. You can choose from many Yatra money offers available online and book your tickets at the most affordable rates.
